Away from machines on the weekend, so I focused on Digital Stuff. I added little simulations of the animation, and generated a couple new designs to make during the week.

reflections & notes

Adding the simulation was a suprisingly big help. It was much quicker to tweak small things, like how quickly I should rotate the shapes. Since I'm not sending machine instructions directly (yet), I found myself dreading the software overhead that the last iterations have required: uploading the svg to GDrive, opening it on the computer connected to the laser cutter's, open the svg in illustrator, press print, open the JobControl software which the laser cutter uses; all to cut my file in less than 30 seconds. And only then can I start taking photos.

Today I put together a design from yesterday, and cut out one from Saturday. Things go quicker now that I have the process under my belt. A few helpful commands to convert file formats, for long-term memory:
  • gif from stills: convert -delay 10 -loop 0 *.png out.gif
  • gif to mp4: ffmpeg -f gif -i in.gif out.mp4
  • loop the mp4 n times: ffmpeg -stream_loop n -i in.mp4 -c copy out.mp4
  • reverse a video/gif: ffmpeg -i in.{format} -vf reverse reversed.{format}

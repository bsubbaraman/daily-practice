Moving away from the spirals, I played around with concentric circles today. Sketch is here.

reflections & notes

It'll be fun to play around with making different components move clockwise/counter-clockwise by changing the ratio between number of frames and spacing around the circle. Another thing I've been missing, compared to creative coding in a purely digital context, is color. Two thoughts on integrating color into the design: if I stack two (or more) pieces of paper in the laser cutter, can I change the material settings to cut through a certain number of layers? Seems like a fire risk and finicky to figure out, but maybe also cool. Alternatively, some designs might permit cutting colored paper to place underneath when photographing; I can also imagine something like a PCB trace router to design additional pieces to cut for the back side of the phenakistoscope. Something to mess around with thi week
